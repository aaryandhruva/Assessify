The problem

Grading hasn't scaled with how we learn.

Manual evaluation of written work is slow, inconsistent, and light on feedback — three problems that compound with every new student.

01

Hours lost to hand-grading

Marking answer scripts by hand is relentless, labor-intensive work that pulls educators away from actually teaching.

02

Inconsistent, biased scores

Fatigue and subjectivity mean the same essay can earn different grades from different markers — or the same one on a different day.

03

Feedback that arrives too late

A bare number tells students little. Without timely, specific guidance, they never learn where to improve.

The platform

An evaluation engine that understands language.

Assessify is a machine-learning platform that removes manual review from subjective and qualitative grading. A natural-language model trained on real answer scripts and rubrics learns what strong work looks like — correct reasoning, sound structure, genuine creativity — and applies it fairly, every single time.

  • Grades essays, open-ended and creative responses — not just multiple choice.
  • Learns from correct answers, common mistakes, structure and originality.
  • Delivers accurate, equitable and repeatable assessments at scale.
